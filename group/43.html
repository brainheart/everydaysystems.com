R,
Those are reasons I mentioned self-publishing. There are some people 
that will be happy to buy somethig in print rather than going a pay 
web site (or even a free one) for the same information. Putting 
something in "book" form, even if that means a spiralbound thing you 

do at Office Max or Staples, does two basic things - it organizes 
your own thoughts about things (if you are any kind of a writer at 
all) and it gives you a tangible, physical product to sell at a 
profit and promote other ideas or products you have.All you need is a 
simple book, a credit card or paypal setup and you're good to go.
